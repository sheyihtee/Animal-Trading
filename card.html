Bulldog

The legs are short, and the head is large with extra skin folds. The nose and ears are dark, and the ears are small and thin.

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Heigth: Male: 31–40 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 8 – 10 years
  • Origin: England

A Bulldog is a medium-sized breed of dog commonly referred to as the English Bulldog or British Bulldog. It is a muscular, hefty dog with a wrinkled face and a distinctive pushed-in nose.
